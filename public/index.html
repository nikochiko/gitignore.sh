$ gitignore.sh

The easiest way to download your gitignore files. Built by @n1kochiko.

Examples

$ curl -o .gitignore gitignore.sh/python
$ curl -JLO gitignore.sh/rust
$ wget --content-disposition gitignore.sh/ocaml
